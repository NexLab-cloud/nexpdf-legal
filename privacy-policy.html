3. How We Use Information

  • To operate and maintain core App functionality
  • To display advertisements that support free use of the App

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information collected by AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google Play Billing is shared with Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. These services may use data to personalize ads, measure ad performance, and improve their own products.

5. Children's Privacy

PDF Tools is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

6. Data Retention

Files you process remain on your device and are managed by you; we do not retain copies. Analytics and crash data are retained by Firebase/Google per their standard retention policies.

7. Your Choices

  • You can opt out of personalized advertising via your device's Ad Settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • You can disable specific permissions (Camera, Storage) at any time in your device's app settings.
  • You can delete the App at any time to remove locally stored data.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
